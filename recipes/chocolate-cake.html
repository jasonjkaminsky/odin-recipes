Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 bar unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/8 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Melt the butter, chocolate, and sugar together in a sauce pan
  2. Place the flour and baking soda in a large bowl
  3. Pour the liquified chocolate sauce into the bowl of flour
  4. Add one egg and one cup milk, and then mix together for 2-3 minutes until smooth
  5. Pour the cake batter into a baking dish and place in oven at 180 degrees C for 35-40 minutes
  6. Enjoy!!!
