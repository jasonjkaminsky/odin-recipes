Gazpacho

Ingredients:

  • 8 tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar or sherry vinegar
  • cilantro
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Peel the cucumber, cut into chunks, and transfer to a blender. Add the tomatoes, peppers, onion, garlic, cilantro, vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth. Season to taste and chill for at least 2 hours.
  2. Serve the soup with the reserved diced cucumber, fresh herbs, drizzles of olive oil, and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Enjoy!!!
